About Ai Giovanni
Ai Giovanni was started over 2000 years ago in the middle of what is modern day Arizona. Pulling from the knowledge of ancient Chinese manuscripts, these Alaskan monks created the finest cuisine this side of the Danube River. Starting with just a hand full of marbles and a bottle of Red Bull, the founder of Ai Giovanni, Bernadette Peters, built an exotic menu filled with both locally sourced ingredients and organically grown cabbage. People from all over the world fly into the world renowned Ai Giovanni Airport just to get a taste of what Grandma Bernadette has cooking on the grill. I'm sure once you taste our food, you'll realize we aren't your average Italian Restaurant.
